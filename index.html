About the Speech to Text Converter

Speech to text is the part of modern technology that allows users to translate voice to text in real time. This technology is based on speech recognition and has great value for people who need to generate a lot of content without getting involved in manual typing. It's also helpful for people who are unable to use keyboards due to some disabilities.

The speech to text converter offered on "Web Speech API" is a application that allows users to convert audio to text. It will assist in minimizing writing efforts significantly. The users can make as many conversions as they need. Our voice recognition tool is used daily by students, teachers, writers, bloggers around the world. Just click the microphone button and start dictating.

HOW TO USE OUR SPEECH TO TEXT CONVERTER ONLINE?

Just follow the simple steps given below:

Step # 1: Click on "Start" button, a pop-up is shown give access to the the Microphone to record speech.

Step # 2: Start speaking in the Microphone the application automatically recognises the speech then convertes that into text.

Step # 3: Click on the "save note" button.

Step # 4: Now your speech is saved to "My voice note" area. if you want to delete that text you can click on the "Delete text" button
